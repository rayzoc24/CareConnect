FEATURE OVERVIEW

One organized civic workflow

CareConnect connects issue reporting, AI classification, NGO claiming, and public feedback in one transparent flow: report, sort, claim, resolve, and show impact.

1. Entry & Authentication

  • Home entry for reporting, browsing, and NGO onboarding
  • Role-based login and signup for volunteers and NGOs
  • Secure profile storage with role-specific access

2. Issue Submission & AI

  • Structured report fields: title, category, location, evidence
  • AI-assisted category and priority suggestions
  • Location tagging using state, district, and area hierarchy

3. Issue Discovery & Action

  • Dashboard filters by location, category, and priority
  • Issue cards for claim, vote, and quick review
  • Detailed view with timeline and evidence before action

4. NGO Operations & Tracking

  • Claim system to avoid duplicate effort
  • Status progression: Pending, Planned, In Progress, Completed
  • NGO task dashboard grouped by live status

5. Community Feedback & Impact

  • Voting to elevate urgent and widely reported issues
  • Post-completion ratings and feedback for accountability
  • Live impact metrics for solved issues and NGO activity
