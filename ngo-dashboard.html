Get Verified

Follow these steps to verify your NGO and start receiving donations.

  1. Prepare your documents: Gather your NGO Registration Certificate (80G/12A), PAN Card, and a recent proof of work/activity report.
  2. Email us: Send these documents to support@careconnect.org from your registered email address.
  3. Review Process: Our team will verify your details within 48-72 hours.
  4. Go Live: Once approved, your profile status will update automatically and you will appear in the donation feeds.
